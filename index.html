About Us

After travelling, we decided to set up an apartment ourselves that you can rent. When travelling usually we look for a small, simple, but clean place that can serve as a basis for our daily excursions.

That is what Vacation Factory Studio is about. A small and clean studio with simple, yet practical furniture. Small doesn't mean uncomfortable, despite its size we do provide a full bed, because we think it's much more comfortable than just a sofa. Choose us if you want a calm place to come back to at the end of the day when you finished your beach time or your exploration of the wonderful French Riviera!

WE BELIEVE IN EVENTFUL VACATIONS

We believe that vacations should be active in order to get the most out of them. Taking our time on the beach is important so that our body gets some rest and recharge. Besides it's also important visit around so that our minds get recharged as well.

Due to its location, our studio provides a perfect origo to plan our vacations on the French Riviera. It's just a few hundred meters away of the spacious rocky beaches of Antibes, but it's also close to the main public transportation lines that help you to visit the region.

Local and inter-city buses, the train station they are all just a couple of minutes away. If you arrive by car, you'll be able to find a parking space around.
